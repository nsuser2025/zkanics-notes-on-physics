#### Deep Potential Long-Range (DPLR)

$$
\begin{align}
E &= \frac{1}{2\pi V} \sum_{m\neq 0, |m| \le L}
\frac{\exp(-\pi^{2}m^{2}/\beta^{2})}{m^{2}} S^{2}(m) \tag{1} \\
S(m) &= \sum_{I} q_{I}\exp(-2 \pi i m R_{I}) + \sum_{n} q_{n} \exp(-2 \pi i m W_{n}) \tag{2}
\end{align}
$$
