#### Inhomogeneous Ornstein-Zernike Equation (IOZ)

<p>
Pair potentialが $u(r_{1},r_{2},\theta_{12})$ のときのIOZ方程式
</p>

$$
\begin{align}
h(r_{1},r_{2},\theta_{12})
&= c(r_{1},r_{2},\theta_{12}) + \int d{\bf r}_{3}
c(r_{1},r_{3},\theta_{13}) ~\rho(r_{3}) h(r_{3},r_{2},\theta_{32}) \tag{1}
\end{align}
$$
